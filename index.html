South African Climate Context

South Africa faces unique climate challenges and opportunities. As one of the world's largest carbon emitters per capita, the country is transitioning toward net-zero emissions by 2050.

  • eThekwini Municipality's commitment to net-zero carbon buildings by 2030
  • South Africa's Climate Change Act and Nationally Determined Contributions
  • Local renewable energy and energy efficiency programs
  • C40 Cities climate action initiatives in Durban
